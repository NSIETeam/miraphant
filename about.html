Miraphant is an application-layer AI company. We don't train models — we ship products that turn frontier intelligence into tools people actually use.

Who we are

Founded in 2026, Miraphant recognized a fundamental gap in the AI industry: frontier models are powerful, but they don't ship products. While labs like Anthropic build increasingly capable brains, someone needs to build the body — the applications, interfaces, and infrastructure that bring intelligence to everyday work.

That's us. We are the application layer. We take the best intelligence available and shape it into products that solve real problems for real people. From Otto, your AI digital colleague, to Circle, a vibrant enterprise service ecosystem — every product we build starts with a question: does this make someone's work life better?

What we do

Ship AI products — fast

Transform organizations into AI-native ones

How we work

We don't do endless meetings. We don't write 200-page specs. We build, we ship, we iterate. Speed is not a shortcut — it's our competitive advantage. When you have an idea, we turn it into a working product before most companies finish scheduling the kickoff call.

And when we deploy inside your organization, we don't just install software. We train your people. Through Miraphant AI Club, every employee — from intern to CEO — learns to command AI agents, accumulate skills, and make efficiency something that stays, even when people leave.

What we believe

We believe AI should augment, not replace. We believe data sovereignty is non-negotiable. We believe the real benchmark is not a leaderboard — it's whether your team got home earlier. We believe safety and shipping are both required, not either-or.

We respect the frontier labs. They build extraordinary brains. Our job is simpler and harder: make sure that intelligence reaches the people who need it.
