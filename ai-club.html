Training Product

Miraphant AI Club

An enterprise AI Native training product for executives and teams. Not a lecture series — a guided transformation program that helps a company pick real scenarios, practice with Otto, build reusable playbooks, and plan controlled rollout.

Why it exists

Most teams know AI is important. Few know what to do next.

AI Club turns abstract interest into usable company capability. Participants leave with real use cases, hands-on experience, a rollout plan, and a first set of reusable work methods.

For leaders

Understand where AI should enter the organization, how to control permissions and cost, and how to measure actual outcomes.

For managers

Choose repeatable department scenarios, build playbooks, and identify which workflows should be standardized first.

For employees

Learn how to ask Otto to complete real work: reports, summaries, follow-ups, research, document processing, and project handover.

Program

A practical AI Native bootcamp

AI awareness reset

Clarify what AI agents can actually do in daily business work, without technical jargon.

Otto hands-on lab

Participants pick business scenarios, assign tasks to Otto, inspect results, and learn how to iterate requests.

Playbook workshop

Turn repeated work into reusable department methods: weekly reports, client follow-up, meeting notes, policy review, and PDF parsing.

PDF and document intelligence

Use the PDF parsing expert to summarize contracts, compare tenders, extract tables, and prepare briefs.

Governance and rollout

Design who can access what, how budgets are allocated, and which departments should pilot first.

ROI review

Review where time is saved, which outputs improve, and how to turn early wins into repeatable adoption.

Deliverables

What the company gets

A prioritized AI use-case list, an Otto practice session, department playbook candidates, PDF/document processing examples, a license and permission rollout draft, and a 30-day adoption plan.
