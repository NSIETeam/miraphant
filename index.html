Who We Are

We're not building
another model company

Founded in 2026, Miraphant recognized a fundamental gap in the AI industry: frontier models are powerful, but they don't ship products. While Anthropic builds the brain, we build the body — the applications, interfaces, and infrastructure that bring intelligence to everyday tools.
Our name fuses mira (wonder, to behold) with the grace and intelligence of the natural world. We create AI that remembers, reasons, and delivers — not as a research project, but as a product you can use.
Our Philosophy

Three principles that define
everything we build

01

Intelligence Must Ship

The best model is worthless if users can't interact with it. We obsess over product experience — every API, every interface, every pixel.

02

Applications Over Benchmarks

We measure success by real-world outcomes, not leaderboard positions. If it works for our users, it works for us.

03

The Layer Above Matters

Infrastructure and models commoditize over time. Value accrues at the application layer — where intelligence meets human needs.
