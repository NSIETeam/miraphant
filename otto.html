Otto's DNA

Four principles that define Otto

ORCHESTRATE

Otto orchestrates models, tools, and APIs into seamless workflows. From code generation to Feishu integration, every capability is composed, not bolted on.

TRUST

Your data stays yours. Otto Enterprise deploys on your LAN with zero data leaving your network. Open-source core, transparent operations, no vendor lock-in.

TRANSFORM

Otto transforms how teams work — replacing fragmented toolchains with a single AI colleague. One assistant for developers, operators, and executives alike.

OUTPUT

Every feature is measured by what it ships. Code deployed, documents generated, tasks automated. Otto optimizes for results, not potential.

Organizational Memory

An AI colleague that remembers how your company works

Otto helps employees reuse the right knowledge, helps managers understand what is working, and helps leaders control access, cost, and outcomes.

Employee work profile

Each employee gets a private work profile: responsibilities, preferences, common tasks, and the best ways Otto should help them.

Project continuity

Every campaign, client delivery, report, hiring plan, contract review, or internal initiative can keep its own shared memory.

Department playbook library

Teams can reuse approved ways of working: report templates, meeting summaries, sales follow-ups, research flows, and document production.

Controlled access

Leaders decide who can use which knowledge, which playbooks, which models, and how much budget each team or project receives.

Project knowledge base

Project knowledge stays available after meetings end: decisions, files, outputs, risks, next steps, and reusable lessons.

Management visibility

Managers see team usage, project progress, cost distribution, repeated work, and which playbooks should be standardized.

For employees: less repeated explanation. For managers: clearer project and team visibility. For leaders: controlled rollout, measurable usage, and reusable company capability. Typical outcomes include faster onboarding, more consistent documents, better project handover, reusable department workflows, and clearer AI spending.

For employees

Otto remembers project background, preferred formats, previous files, and common tasks, so employees can ask for a report, proposal, follow-up message, meeting summary, or data table without explaining everything again.

For department leads

Leads can see which projects are active, where time and AI budget are going, which work is repeated often, and which team methods should become approved templates.

For company owners

Owners can roll out AI safely: assign licenses, control who can use company knowledge, monitor spending, and turn proven team workflows into company-wide capability.

Example uses: onboarding a new hire with department knowledge, preparing weekly reports from previous formats, keeping client delivery context across weeks, standardizing event review documents, reusing approved sales follow-up flows, and seeing which teams produce the best AI-assisted outcomes.
